⚡ AttentionX
  AI-Powered Automated Content Repurposing Enginr transforms long-form videos into viral short-form clips with emotional peak detection, dynamic captions, and smart vertical cropping — all powered by AI.
  
🔥Overview
Content creators spend hours manually rewatching footage, identifying engaging moments, clipping segments, reframing to vertical, and adding captions.
AttentionX automates the entire pipeline, turning long videos into ready-to-publish short-form content in minutes.

🚀 What It Does
  📤 Upload long-form videos
  🎙️ AI transcribes audio with word-level timestamps
  🎯 Detects emotional peaks & high-engagement moments
  ✂️ Automatically generates short clips
  📱 Exports vertical (9:16) videos with captions


✨ Features
  Emotional Peak Detection
  AI analyzes transcript sentiment and narrative flow to identify viral-worthy moments
  Dynamic Captions
  Choose from 3 styles:
    🎤 Karaoke (word-by-word highlight)
    💪 Bold (sentence emphasis)
    ✨ Minimal (clean subtitles)
  Timestamp-Based Highlights: Clips include precise start/end times with emotion labels & sentiment scores
  Smart Vertical Crop: Automatically converts clips to 9:16 format for TikTok, Reels & Shorts
  Phone Mockup Preview: Preview clips inside a realistic mobile frame
  Clip Export & Download: Export MP4 videos with burned captions + downloadable SRT files
Session Resumption: Resume processing even after closing the tab
Real-Time Pipeline UI: Live progress tracking with animated processing stages

🧠 AI Pipeline
Upload → Transcription → Peak Detection → Clip Generation → Captions & Thumbnails → ✅ Ready
